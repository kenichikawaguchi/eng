NARRATOR: You will hear a conversation between a shopkeeper and a customer discussing a new environmental initiative.
A: Good morning! I saw the sign about your new reusable bag policy.
B: Ah, yes! We're very excited about it. Starting next week, we'll be charging a small fee for single-use plastic bags.
A: Interesting. What’s the reasoning behind this change?
B: Well, our aim is to significantly reduce plastic waste in the community. We hope this encourages more people to bring their own bags.
A: I understand. And where does the revenue from the bag fee go?
B: All proceeds will be donated to local environmental charities focused on coastal clean-ups. We believe in investing back into our planet.
A: That’s a commendable approach. Are there any exceptions to the fee?
B: For now, no. We want to make a clear statement. We’ll also be offering a discount on our reusable bags for the first month to help customers transition.
A: That sounds like a well-thought-out plan. I’ll definitely bring my own bag from now on.
B: We appreciate your support! Every little bit helps, and we’re hopeful this will make a real difference.
QUESTION: What is the primary purpose of the shop's new policy regarding plastic bags?
CHOICE: A. To increase the shop's profit margin.
CHOICE: B. To encourage customers to purchase more reusable bags.
CHOICE: C. To reduce the amount of plastic waste generated by the shop.
CHOICE: D. To comply with a new government regulation.
ANSWER: The correct answer is C.
